Here Are Some Affordable Make-up Brands You Can Switch Too:




Elf


This cosmetics company uses only animal-free materials. All its brushes and false eyelashes are 100 percent synthetic. Animal hair is never used. The company has never conducted, commissioned, or paid for tests on animals anywhere in the world.






CoverGirl




Covergirl does not test on animals and are Leaping Bunny certified by Cruelty Free International, which means they’re Cruelty Free across ALL of their products, EVERYWHERE they’re sold.






Milani




Milani has confirmed that it is truly cruelty-free. They don't test finished products or ingredients on animals, and neither do their suppliers or any third-parties.
